Cars and Racing

This is the big one. Cars are not just a hobby for me, it's the thing I think about every single day. I watch every F1 race, I know way too much about lap times and tire compounds, and I've been following GT racing since I was like 13.

My dream is to drive on the Spa-Francorchamps circuit one day. Eau Rouge, the weather that changes every 5 minutes, the elevation... just watching onboards of that track gives me chills. I don't care if it's a track day in a rental or if I somehow end up racing there for real, I just want to feel what it's like to go through Eau Rouge flat out. That's the goal.

Entrepreneurship

I came to IESEG because I want to build my own thing. I don't know what yet, I don't have a big startup idea ready to go, but I know I want to be my own boss. Right now I'm just absorbing everything: marketing, finance, strategy. The idea will come, and when it does I want to be ready.

Food

I'll be honest, my taste in food is not exactly refined. I live for fast food. Nashville hot chicken, smash burgers, loaded fries... if it's greasy and spicy I'm there. Tasty Crousty is my go-to, I probably go there way too often. People say Lille is great for restaurants and fine dining but honestly I'm perfectly happy with a good chicken sandwich and a sprite at 11pm.
